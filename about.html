About Me

I specialize in creating custom software solutions that streamline your workflow and eliminate tedious, repetitive tasks. My passion is problem-solving, and I find great satisfaction in helping businesses save time and increase efficiency.

My journey into software development was driven by a desire to find a better way to work. I discovered the power of automation when I transformed a 90-minute administrative task into a 2-minute automated process. This experience showed me the immense potential of software to revolutionize how we work.

I believe in transparent and fair pricing. My goal is to provide you with a solution that pays for itself in saved time and resources. I offer a unique pricing model where the cost of the software is based on the time it saves you, with a one-year payment plan. After that, the software is yours to keep, with no recurring subscription fees.

I am currently developing a suite of tools that I hope you will find useful. If you have a project in mind or are interested in learning more about how I can help you work less and live more, I would love to hear from you.
